About Us - Leo Taxi Service

For the past 8 years, we have proudly served as a leading taxi service company in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. With a fleet of over 600 cabs at our disposal, we have been dedicated to providing reliable and comfortable transportation solutions to our valued customers.

At Leo Taxi Service, we understand that getting from point A to point B efficiently and safely is of utmost importance. That's why we have assembled a team of skilled and professional drivers who are committed to delivering exceptional service every step of the way.

Whether you need a ride to the airport, a local destination, or a long-distance journey, we've got you covered. Our diverse range of vehicles ensures that we can accommodate any group size or travel requirements, making us the go-to choice for individuals, families, and businesses alike.

Customer satisfaction is at the heart of our operations, and we continually strive to exceed expectations. We prioritize punctuality, reliability, and affordability, ensuring that our customers experience a seamless and enjoyable journey with us.

Booking a cab with us is a breeze! Simply give us a call or use our user-friendly mobile app to reserve your ride. Our efficient dispatch system ensures prompt service, getting you to your destination on time.

We owe our success and reputation to the trust and support of our loyal customers. We are grateful for the opportunity to serve the Vellore community and look forward to many more years of providing top-notch transportation services.
